## Part 3: The Core Functional Job for DentalTechHub

### Job Statement

> **"Selecting the right technology solution to address a specific practice need"**

This job is performed by **multiple executors** (Practice Owner, Office Manager, DSO Executive, DSO Procurement Specialist) but follows a **common process** regardless of who does it.

### Job Map: 12 Chronological Steps

| Step | Job Step Statement |
|------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Define** the operational or clinical problem requiring a technology solution |
| 2 | **Determine** the requirements and constraints for an acceptable solution |
| 3 | **Identify** the category of technology that addresses the need |
| 4 | **Discover** available solutions in the market |
| 5 | **Screen** solutions against basic requirements to create a shortlist |
| 6 | **Evaluate** shortlisted solutions in depth (features, pricing, reviews, demos) |
| 7 | **Validate** solution fit through references, trials, or proof-of-concept |
| 8 | **Compare** finalists and make a selection decision |
| 9 | **Negotiate** terms, pricing, and contracts with the selected vendor |
| 10 | **Approve** the purchase through internal stakeholders or budget holders |
| 11 | **Implement** the selected solution into practice operations |
| 12 | **Assess** whether the solution delivered expected value post-implementation |

## Part 8: DentalTechHub Value Opportunity Analysis

### Highest-Value Job Steps for Marketplace Innovation

Based on the desired outcomes analysis, **DentalTechHub has the highest opportunity to create value in Job Steps 4-8**:

| Job Step | Opportunity Level | Why High Opportunity |
|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Step 4: Discover** | 🔴 Very High | Fragmented information, biased sources, no central hub |
| **Step 5: Screen** | 🔴 Very High | Manual process, inconsistent criteria, time-consuming |
| **Step 6: Evaluate** | 🔴 Very High | Hidden pricing, outdated info, hard to compare, fake reviews |
| **Step 7: Validate** | 🟠 High | Hard to find references, vendor-controlled trial access |
| **Step 8: Compare** | 🟠 High | No apples-to-apples tools, analysis paralysis |

| Job Step | Opportunity Level | Why Lower |
|----------|-------------------|-----------|

| Step 9 | 🟡 Medium | Negotiation is vendor-buyer relationship; marketplace can provide benchmarks |
| Steps 10-12 | 🟢 Lower | Implementation/assessment happen outside marketplace scope |
