Recommendation: consolidate the three analytics vendors onto Vendor A this quarter — it cuts cost ~46% with the lowest migration effort and covers every current use case.

Why now

All three contracts renew in Q3. Acting before renewal avoids auto-renewal lock-in and gives a clean migration window. Waiting another cycle compounds the overlap cost and the maintenance burden of three integrations.

Risks

Migration risk detail
